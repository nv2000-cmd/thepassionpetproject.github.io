The Passion Pet Project

Who Are We?

The Passion Pet Project is a local, teen-led nonprofit that collects extra pet necessities (like leashes, collars, bedding, toys, food, and grooming supplies) to donate to county shelters.

Our mission is to help pets in shelters feel more comfortable. Every pet deserves love—and every small act of care can make a big difference.

Our Why

The Passion Pet Project was founded by 16-year-old Neylan Vily to bring comfort to pets living in difficult shelter conditions. Inspired by the rescue stories of our own adopted animals, we know we can’t save them all, but we believe every small act of care makes a big difference.
